A collection of recular expressions that have been useful, for future reference.
To use this, you need to you "Replace in Files" in VS, and make sure that Regular Expressions
are selected under Find Options.

---
Find and replaces occurances of resources.Lookup(blah) with blah.Get(resources):
Find: resources.Lookup\({[^)]*}\)
Replace with: \1.Get(resources)
Note: This will not play nice if the <blah> expression has parentheses of its own.
